---

Key Takeaways:

- Blogging is not the best channel for building an audience anymore.
- Social media, particularly LinkedIn, is the best place to build your personal brand.
- Trust and performance are damaged with generic content created by agencies.
- Practitioner-led content vastly outperforms agency-created content.
- Great content comes from practitioners, not hired freelance writers.
- Customer research is key to positioning and product messaging.
- Founder led content significantly speeds up business growth.

Relevance to LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy:

Content Creation

- Share personal insights and experiences.
- Create content based on your expertise and experience.
- Produce practitioner-led content.

Positioning

- Research the target audience's pain points.
- Test your messaging with the intended customers.
- Find your unique positioning.

Growth

- Use consistent thought leadership to build authority.
- Have founders create the content for maximum engagement.
- The key to a successful brand is audience trust, not volume of content.

Distribution

- Use LinkedIn as your main audience-building channel.
- Use further insights in blogs, newsletters, podcasts, etc.

Conclusion:

To succeed as a B2B creator, you need to have real expertise and consistently publish unique insights based on your understanding of customer needs.
